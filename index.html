In Begginner Lever, one symbol is shown as both halves of the body will execute the movement at the same time.

Click on the symbol on the left for a video demonstration.

↓

Moving away from the starting position in a fluid manner

⇣

Moving away from the starting position in a sharp manner

↑

Moving toward from the starting position in a fluid manner

⇡

Moving toward from the starting position in a sharp manner

▼

Arrive at the bottom of the range of motion

▲

Arrive at the top of the range of motion

-

No movement / freeze / hold. Could be at top or bottom of a range of motion

B

Bounce twice within one beat at the bottom of the range of motion

⇳

Complete full reps at your own pace until the designated time is over

⇔

Alternation each side of the body, Complete full reps at your own pace until the designated time is over

✸

BURN OUT! Complete full reps as fast as you can until the designated time is over
